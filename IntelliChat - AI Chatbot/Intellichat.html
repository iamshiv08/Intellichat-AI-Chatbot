Introducing Intellichat

I’ve trained a model called INTELLICHAT which interacts in a conversational way. The dialogue format makes it possible for INTELLICHAT to answer follow up questions, admit its mistakes, challenge incorrect premises, and reject inappropriate requests. INTELLICHAT is a sibling model to InstructGPT⁠, which is trained to follow an instruction in a prompt and provide a detailed response. We are excited to introduce INTELLICHAT to get users’ feedback and learn about its strengths and weaknesses. During the research preview, usage of INTELLICHAT is free. Try it now at intallichat.com⁠(opens in a new window).
